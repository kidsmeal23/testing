Our Services

  1. Technology Assessment: FirNova evaluates the feasibility and readiness of the technology developed in the lab for scaling up and commercialization. This involves analyzing the technology's potential, capabilities, and compatibility with the intended market.
  2. Market Strategy: Early in the process, FirNova conducts market research to identify target customers, competitors, and market trends. They use this information to develop a comprehensive market strategy that guides the product's design and features to meet market needs.
  3. Process Development: FirNova works on developing scalable and efficient manufacturing processes that can be used to produce the product on a larger scale. They focus on optimizing the production methods to ensure consistency and cost-effectiveness.
  4. Prototyping and Testing: FirNova creates prototypes of the product based on the lab results. These prototypes are tested to verify performance, functionality, and reliability. Feedback from testing is used to refine the product design.
  5. Sensitivity Analysis: Sensitivity analysis involves evaluating how changes in variables, such as production cost, material costs, labor expenses, and market demand, impact the feasibility, profitability, and viability of the product. It helps identify potential risks and uncertainties associated with the project.
  6. Environmental Impact Assessment: An assessment is conducted to evaluate the potential environmental impact of the product's manufacturing, use, and disposal. This evaluation helps in identifying ways to minimize the product's ecological footprint and improve its sustainability.
  7. Intellectual Property Protection: FirNova assists in protecting the technology or product through intellectual property rights such as patents, trademarks, or copyrights. This safeguarding ensures exclusive rights and prevents unauthorized use or copying.
  8. Pilot Scale-Up: The pilot scale-up involves transitioning from lab-scale production to a larger pilot-scale production to further test and validate the product's performance and manufacturing processes.
  9. Design and Engineering: FirNova provides detailed engineering designs and specifications not only for the commercial product but also for the overall plant design. This includes layout, equipment selection, and process integration.
  10. Regulatory Compliance: FirNova guides the client through regulatory requirements and compliance for both the product and the plant design to meet industry standards and gain necessary certifications for market approval.
  11. Quality Control and Assurance: FirNova implements quality control measures not only for the product but also for the plant design to maintain consistency and ensure that both meet the desired specifications and quality standards.
  12. Supplier and Manufacturer Selection: Assistance is provided in identifying reliable suppliers and manufacturers for both the product and the plant equipment, ensuring smooth supply chain operations and seamless integration.
  13. Performance Optimization: Performance optimization is an ongoing process that involves continuously fine-tuning both the product design and the plant processes to enhance performance, efficiency, and overall competitiveness in the market.
  14. Project Management: The consultant oversees the entire scaling-up process, coordinating with different stakeholders to ensure a successful transition to commercial production, including the construction and commissioning of the plant.
  15. Training and Support: Training and support are provided to the client's team during the scaling-up process and the operation of the commercial plant to ensure a seamless integration of the new product and processes into their operations.
